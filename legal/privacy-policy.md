## What information we collect and why

### 1. When you use the Interview Coach (coach.aievolvingyou.com)

The Interview Coach uses magic-link sign-in so we can manage your credits, purchases, and access to your sessions.

**What we collect:**
- Your email address — to send your sign-in link and identify your account
- Your job role, career stage, and job description — entered by you to generate tailored questions
- Your answers to interview questions, and the questions, coaching feedback, and cheat sheet generated from them
- Session completion status and credits used — to manage your account and access

When you use the Interview Coach, the role information, job description, and answers you provide are sent to our AI model provider so the service can generate questions, coaching feedback, and your cheat sheet. See the third-party processors table below.

**How long we keep it:**
Your session content is retained in our database so you can access your session history. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting man@aievolvingyou.com. We will action deletion requests within 30 days.

### 2. When you purchase a product

Payments are processed by Stripe. We do not see or store your card details — ever. Stripe handles all payment information directly.

We do not receive or store your card number. Stripe may share limited transaction information with us, such as payment confirmation, product purchased, customer email address, and transaction reference, so we can fulfil your purchase and provide support. Stripe's own privacy policy governs how they handle your payment data: stripe.com/gb/privacy

**Contact form:** If you contact us via the website, we collect your name, email address, and message. We use this only to respond to your enquiry.

## Your rights under UK GDPR

You have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you

- **Delete** your data (the right to be forgotten)

To exercise any of these rights, email us at man@aievolvingyou.com. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are not satisfied with our response, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.
