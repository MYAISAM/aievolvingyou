## AI Skills Library category cards

### Start Here: AI Basics

For beginners who want a plain-English introduction to AI, generative AI, common tools and everyday use cases.

Suggested CTA:

> Start learning AI

### Prompting & Productivity

For people who want to use AI better at work: writing, summarising, planning, researching, analysing and automating small tasks.

Suggested CTA:

> Build practical AI habits

### AI for Business Leaders

For managers, founders and leaders who need to understand AI strategy, adoption, governance and team impact.

Suggested CTA:

> Lead AI adoption better

### AI for HR & Recruitment

For recruiters, HR teams and people leaders who need to understand AI in hiring, screening, candidate experience and workforce planning.

Suggested CTA:

> Explore AI for people teams

### AI for Marketing & Content

For marketers, copywriters, content creators and comms teams adapting to AI-generated content and workflow automation.

Suggested CTA:

> Upgrade your content workflow

### AI for Sales & Customer Teams

For sales reps, account managers, SDRs and customer success teams using AI for research, outreach, follow-up and customer support.

Suggested CTA:

> Learn AI for customer work

### AI for Finance & Operations

For finance analysts, operations teams, admin professionals, PMOs and coordinators using AI for reporting, documentation and process improvement.

Suggested CTA:

> Improve everyday operations

### AI for Developers & Builders

For engineers, technical builders, no-code builders and product-focused learners who want to build with LLMs and AI tools.

Suggested CTA:

> Build with AI

### AI for Educators & Trainers

For teachers, trainers, instructional designers and L&D teams using AI to design learning and teach AI literacy.

Suggested CTA:

> Teach and design with AI

### Responsible AI & Ethics

For people who need to understand safe, fair, transparent and responsible AI use.

Suggested CTA:

> Learn responsible AI

### AI Displacement & Future of Work

For people trying to understand how AI may change jobs, tasks, skills and workforce structures.

Suggested CTA:

> Understand the shift
